The Diagnosis

You built your book on referrals. Referrals do not scale on command.

Your first few clients came from people who already knew your work. That well does not refill itself.

Most fractional operators respond by posting more. More content, more comments, more connection requests. What comes back is peer engagement. Other consultants. Not buyers.

The problem is not visibility. Your content and your pipeline are two separate systems, and neither one is built to find a buyer before they start looking.

For M&A advisors and business brokers, the same gap shows up as a revenue problem, not a content one. You do not get paid for effort, you get paid when a deal closes, and every quiet month is a real, calculable gap. Referral relationships help, until the well runs dry mid-quarter with no fallback and no way to tell who is actually close to selling.

What is an active signal, and how does it find my ICP?

A signal is a verifiable event indicating a buyer is currently in a situation where they are likely to need what you sell. For fractional CFOs and COOs: a recent funding round, a finance-team leadership change, a hiring trend. For M&A advisors and business brokers: finance-department hiring buildouts, cost-cutting activity, ownership or succession changes, signs of contraction. These are live, verifiable events, not a static purchased list.

Can this be customised to my practice?

Yes. Once it is installed on your own account, you control the filters, the pace, and the mix entirely. It is built around your specific ICP from the start, not flexed within someone else’s fixed package.

How is this different from a LinkedIn ghostwriter or a growth agency?

A ghostwriter writes posts. The audience problem stays untouched. A growth agency runs your account through an account manager, often with automation that puts your LinkedIn account at risk, and locks you into a contract at $1,500 to $7,000 a month for as long as you keep paying.

This is a one-time build you own outright. Direct access to the person who built it, no automation, and when the engagement with me ends, the system does not stop working.
