Frequently asked
How does billing work?
PayPal auto-bills $697 on the same date each month. You receive a receipt automatically. Cancel anytime from your PayPal account, no notice period required.
When does my first delivery arrive?
Within 7 days of onboarding. Onboarding is a 90-minute session where we lock your voice, ICP filters, and positioning. After that, every Monday at 9 AM your local time.
How much time does this take from me each week?
Roughly 30 minutes per week. You review the Monday package, approve or request edits on the posts, schedule them via LinkedIn's native scheduler, and send connection requests from your own account. Everything else is handled.
Can I adjust what gets delivered each month?
Yes. The standard mix is 12 posts, 2 articles, 2 carousels, and 2 infographics. If a different mix works better for your practice, deliverables flex to fit at no extra cost. Request changes any time via Slack or WhatsApp.
