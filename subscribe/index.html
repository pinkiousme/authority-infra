How does billing work?
PayPal auto-bills $697 on the same date each month. You receive a receipt automatically. Cancel anytime from your PayPal account — no notice period, no exit fees.
When does my first delivery arrive?
Within 7 days of onboarding. Onboarding is a 90-minute session where we lock your voice, ICP filters, and positioning. After that, every Monday at 9 AM your local time.
Custom pricing available?
Yes. If your practice has different volume needs, regional focus, or specialised deliverable requirements, custom pricing can be arranged. Book a 30-minute call with Saurabh to discuss what fits your engagement: calendly.com/saurabh_zentro/30-min or email saurabh@pipelind.com.
How much time does this take from me each week?
Roughly 30 minutes per week: ~20 minutes Sunday to schedule posts, ~10 minutes Monday to send 10 connection requests. Everything else is handled.
Can I adjust the deliverables?
Yes, at no extra cost. The standard mix is 12 posts + 2 articles + 2 carousels + 2 infographics. If a different mix works better for your practice, deliverables flex to fit. Request changes anytime via email.
