The Handraiser Post
Lead generation, run once every 2 to 3 weeks
I put together [a specific checklist, template, or breakdown] that [the exact outcome it delivers]. It covers: [Specific thing 1] [Specific thing 2] [Specific thing 3] It's free. Comment "[ONE WORD]" and I'll send it over. (Must be a connection so I can message you, feel free to connect first.)

04 The comment-to-call script

When someone comments on your Handraiser post, this 3-message sequence delivers the asset, starts a real conversation, and earns the call without pitch-slapping. Send message 1 immediately, message 2 only after they reply, message 3 only if their reply shows the pain.

Message 1, deliver, then one question
Send immediately
Hey [name], here's the [asset] you asked for: [link] Quick question while I have you: are you [situation A, e.g. "already tracking this yourself"] or [situation B, e.g. "just starting to look into it"]?
The either/or question gets far more replies than "let me know if you have questions," because it costs nothing to answer.
Message 2, give value on their answer
Send after they reply
That makes sense. The thing I see most often at that stage is [the specific obstacle], [one genuinely useful tip, 1 to 2 sentences, no pitch]. Out of curiosity, what's your current process for [the relevant task]?
You're diagnosing, not selling. Their answer here tells you whether they're a real prospect before you ask for anything.
Message 3, the earned ask
Only if their reply shows the pain
Got it, that's exactly the pattern I work on with [your ICP]. Happy to walk through how I'd approach it for your situation specifically. No charge, no deck, 30 minutes, you leave with a plan either way: [your booking link]
"You leave with a plan either way" removes the risk of saying yes. If message 2 showed no real pain, skip this and stay helpful.
