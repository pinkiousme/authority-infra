Finance pattern insight · designed to establish authority with CEOs and founders at scaling companies
Most founders think the finance function build starts with the CFO hire. It does not. The CFO hire solves the capacity problem. It does not solve the architecture problem. The companies that scale finance cleanly start with the structure: what the forecasting model needs to do, how COGS gets allocated across product lines, what management reporting has to tell the board.
