Most growing businesses do not slow down because of bad hires. They slow down because growth entered a structure that was already carrying debt. Not financial debt. Operational debt. The kind that accumulates when processes are not documented, when accountability lives in relationships rather than systems, when decisions that should be handled at the floor level keep escalating to the owner.
